Brief biography

A little about my journey

I grew up taking apart the family desktop just to see how it went back together — which, in hindsight, was the first sign I'd end up here. I started at MIVA Open University to formalize what I'd been teaching myself online, and I've since developed a genuine love for the logic of front-end development: the way a good layout, clear code, and a bit of design sense come together to make something people actually enjoy using.

Outside coursework, I'm the kind of student who keeps a running list of small projects — this portfolio being one of them. I'm especially interested in how web technologies can support academic life itself, which is why the Academic Planner on this site isn't a token feature; it's the tool I use to track my own assignments.
